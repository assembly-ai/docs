### Analytics 101

#### General

Without customization, you can view the following for you entire organization:

- Total interactions
    - Total bugs
    - Total feature requests
    - Total questions asked
- Total escalations 
- Total resolutions
- Average response time (per day, week, month, or range)
- Average response time per customer channel (slack, email, etc)
