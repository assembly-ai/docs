## CRM & Customer Data

Connect your CRM systems to enrich customer context with account data, contacts, opportunities, and more.

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion
    icon={
      <img
        style={{ height: '1.25rem' }}
        src="integrations/salesforce.svg"
      />
    }
    title="Salesforce"
  >
    **Connect your Salesforce account to see customer data in Assembly.**
    
    <b>Enable the Salesforce integration</b>
    <br/>
    On the Manage Integrations page, click on the Salesforce integration and hit the <b>enable</b> button. Follow the sign-in instructions to connect Salesforce to Assembly.
    <br/>
    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="images/SFDC_Enabling.png"
    />
    <br/>
    <b>Select which Salesforce objects to sync</b>
    <br/>
    After enabling the integration, Assembly will present a table mapping step where you can choose which Salesforce objects you want to bring into Assembly. You can map <b>Accounts</b>, <b>Contacts</b>, <b>Opportunities</b>, and any <b>custom objects</b> from Salesforce.
    <br/>
    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="images/Auto-mapping_SFDC.png"
    />
    <br/>
    <b>Review how objects map into Assembly</b>
    <br/>
    The Salesforce objects you select will be mapped to their Assembly equivalents: <b>Accounts → Accounts</b>, <b>Contacts → Contacts</b>, <b>Opportunities → Deals</b>, and <b>custom objects → custom objects</b> in Assembly. Review these mappings to ensure they match how your team works.
    <br/>
    <img
      style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
      src="images/Select_SFDC_Fields.png"
    />
    <br/>
    <b>Use auto-mapping for fields</b>
    <br/>
    You can use Assembly's auto-mapping to quickly align common Salesforce fields (such as name, owner, status, and amounts) to Assembly fields. Adjust any individual field mappings as needed before saving to finalize your Salesforce integration.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion
    icon={
      <img
        style={{ height: '1.25rem' }}
        src="integrations/hubspot.svg"
      />
    }
    title="HubSpot"
  >
    **Connect your HubSpot account to see customer data in Assembly.**
    
    Sync your HubSpot CRM data including contacts, companies, and deals into Assembly. This enables your team to see the full customer picture when handling support requests and conversations.

    - Import HubSpot contacts and companies
    - Sync deal stages and pipeline data
    - Map HubSpot properties to Assembly fields
    - Enable customer context in conversations
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
